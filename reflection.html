Reflection

The Glaze is a restaurant concept I created to focus on bold flavors and comfort food. I wanted to design something that feels both welcoming and high quality.

Throughout this project, I learned how to build and structure a website using HTML and CSS. I practiced organizing content, linking pages together, and styling elements to make the site look clean and professional.

One of the most important things I learned was how to properly upload files and connect them to my site, like images and videos. This helped me understand how websites actually function behind the scenes.

In the future, I would like to continue improving this website by adding more pages, better styling, and interactive features. This project helped me feel more confident in building websites.
