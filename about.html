About Me

Hi, my name is Addy. This is The Glaze. For as long as I can remember, I have found my joy in the kitchen beside my grnadmother growing up. Learning from her the patience and creativness behind a perfect sauce. Those weekends turned into a lifelong passion for the art of flavor. After years of cooking, I created the project that this is: The Glaze. Every dish on our menu is a blend of those roots, the warmth of home cooking and boldness of new ides. This is not just food to me; it's a way of sharing my story, one glaze at a time.

My Skills

I mostly specialize in developing flavorful bases for sauces. Much of my cooking comes from my grandmother who traveled all throughout Europe and Spain to learn cooking. My biggest project was cooking for a 15 person event for my Dad's business. I spent four days marinating meats and making homeade broths with scraps. So much can go wrong in the kitchen. Things get burnt, flavors dont mix, and plating gets easily messed up. Cooking has taught me that patience during the toughest challenges is one of the most important traits to have, especially as a chef. I would love to pursue my career in cooking and have an opportunity to open up a resturant of my own.
